What is Salah?

Salah, or Namaz, refers to the five daily prayers obligatory for every Muslim. These prayers, offered at specific times, are a spiritual connection fostering discipline, mindfulness, and faith.

Daily Salah Timings in Bradford

salah bradford Prayer timings vary daily due to daylight changes. The five daily prayers include:

  • Fajr (Dawn Prayer): Just before sunrise.
  • Zuhr (Noon Prayer): After the sun reaches its zenith.
  • Asr (Afternoon Prayer): Midway between noon and sunset.
  • Maghrib (Evening Prayer): Just after sunset.
  • Isha (Night Prayer): After twilight disappears.

Nawafil (Voluntary Prayers) in Bradford

Nawafil prayers deepen spiritual connection and include:

  • Tahajjud: After midnight, before Fajr.
  • Ishraq: 15-20 minutes after sunrise.
  • Chasht (Duha): Mid-morning, before Zuhr.
  • Awwabin: After Maghrib, before Isha.
